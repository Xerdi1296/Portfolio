Burnart

My history with Burnart began in October 2021 when I started my one month internship. During that month I learned how to professionaly edit videos in Adobe Premiere Pro and I also learned the basics of Adobe After Effects.
​My first tasks consisted of editing short vlogs and few corporate videos.

After that I started working for the Burnart and I worked on more advanced projects. Below you will find two videos I worked on for Burnart. ​First is a 2022 season preview of Strzegom Horse Trials. Second video is a short reportage about Ball Hockey European Championship which took place in Bratislava in 2019.



Working for Burnart also allowed me to gain some experience on the set and I had an opportunity to use my sound design experience to record a voice over which was needed for the video we were working on. When my experience grew, I also worked on a few commercials and more corporate videos.





Goel Ha-Dam


In 2019 album called "Goel" was released on Spotify. It was a project which I worked on with my friends. I was responsible for recording, mixing and mastering all songs that are available in this album. I also recorded all of the songs released by band Goel-ha-dam.

"Bez Ciebie" is a single which I arranged, recorded, mixed and mastered. It was the last song I worked on as part of Goel project.
