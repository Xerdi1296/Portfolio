A little about myself



My name is Maciej Mikoda and I am a sound designer, composer and video editor. I am a graduate of University of Silesia in Cieszyn where I got an MA in video game sound design.

​I have a huge passion for games, especially for audio and music which, I developed over the years by working on different projects both professionally and while I was in college.

In this website you will find my best works which I worked on as sound designer, composer and video editor.




Sound Design

My current experience as sound designer consists of creating and implemeting sounds for a released game on Steam, artistic works which were part of Batchelor's and Master's thesis and other projects which I worked on for last 7 years. For every project I try to minimise usage of audio libraries and use my own sounds to ensure originality of sound design throughout entire project.

Music

For music composition I use FL Studio and Reaper if I create a score for videos. I have access to high quality libraries of virtual instruments of top brands such as East West and Native Instruments.

Video Editing

My current experience in video editing consists of working on various projects. I have worked on trailers of YouTube channels, corporate videos, vlogs and compilation/music videos. My main video editing software is Davinci Resolve but I also have access to Adobe Premiere Pro.

3D Art

I started creatig 3d models while I was in college and I've been developing my skills after I graduated. I mainly create 3d models in 3ds Max but I also know basics of Blender.
