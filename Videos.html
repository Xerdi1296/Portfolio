Video Editing

I started editing videos in 2015. For last 7 years I have gained some experience while working on various projects both professionally and as part of collaboration with YouTbe channels. Below you will find examples of videos I worked on as video eidor. Most of videos available on this website were edited in Adobe Premiere Pro but I also edit on Davinci Resolve. Apart from video editing I also had an opportunity to appear in a music video made by "Silent Choir" of Universtiy of Silesia. My appearence in this video was part of collaboration with "Silent Choir" in 2019.

Burnart

My history with Burnart began in October 2021 when I started my one month internship. During that month I learned how to professionaly edit videos in Adobe Premiere Pro and I also learned the basics of Adobe After Effects.
​My first tasks consisted of editing short vlogs and few corporate videos.

After that I started working for the Burnart and I worked on more advanced projects. Below you will find two videos I worked on for Burnart. ​First is a 2022 season preview of Strzegom Horse Trials. Second video is a short reportage about Ball Hockey European Championship which took place in Bratislava in 2019.



Working for Burnart also allowed me to gain some experience on the set and I had an opportunity to use my sound design experience to record a voice over which was needed for the video we were working on. When my experience grew, I also worked on a few commercials and more corporate videos.

Gung Ho Vids

I started editing videos for Gung Ho Vids in 2015. First video I edited for this channel was short 60 second trailer. After that video was released our collaboration advanced and I worked on other projects. Most of the videos I worked on were short compilations which showed what type of content is available on Gung Ho Vids. In 2021 we started creating new type of videos. These films were more focused on specific type of vehicle or certain branch of the US military.

This 60 second trailer shows the type of content which is available on Gung Ho Vids. This was the first video I editied for this channel in 2015.

This 3 minute compilation is an extended version of original trailer of the channel which was released in 2015. It's less dynamic which allows viewers to explore and see what type of conent is available on Gung Ho Vids channel. This is the most popular video I worked on for Gung Ho Vids.

University of Silesia

Apart from YouTube channels I also worked on videos made by silent choir „Niemy Chór Uniwersytetu Śląskiego” of University of Silesia. My collaboration with silent choir began in 2019 when I was asked to record singers for music video of a song „Merry Christmas Everoyone”. After that video was released I also worked on other projects which were made by silent choir of University of Silesia.





First video was made before Christmas in 2019. In case of this video I was responsible for recording singers in the studio and mixing and mastering recorded audio tracks.

Second video was released in May 2020. My contribution for this clip was small, because I only shortened the music to fit the length of the video.




Since begining of March 2022 both videos are not available to watch on YouTube. Because of that I put an article about one of the videos which is available on the website of University of Silesia. The main idea of the video was to express graditute to all man and women who were involved in the fight with Covid-19 epidemic. Song used in this clip is made by Polish artist Filip Lato.
