Sound Design and Music

This section consists of games and other projects I wokred on as sound designer and composer. My main software for creating sounds and music is FL Studio but I also work with Reaper.

Forever Skies

In June 2022 I had an opportunity to support the team of Far From Home which is a small indie developer based in Poland. My job was to create sound effects for their newest game: Forever Skies. Game will be available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S and PC later this year.

Truck Simulator Pro Europe

In April 2022 I joined Mageeks which is a small studio based in Warsaw. One of their games I worked on as a sound designer is Truck Simulator Pro Europe. My task was to create engine sounds for all trucks available in the game. Sounds I created were available to hear in update 2.4 released in June 2022 and they are still in the game.

Pest Control

Pest Control is my first completed game which I worked on as sound designer and composer. I was responsible for creating and implementation of all music and sound effects via Wwise engine. I was a part of the team which worked on this game for two months. During that time, I completed my part of the developing process. Pest Control is now available on Steam.

Master's Thesis - Space of Isolation

"Space of Isolation" is a short video game created to demonstrate that music and sound effects in game can exist as an independent musical work. All sounds and music in this game were implemented with Audiokinetic's Wwise audio engine.

Most of sound effects used in this project were recorded or synthesised by me. I also used some sounds from audio libraries. These sounsd were heavily modified to keep consistant style of audio throughout entire project. In order of achievieng desired effect of sounds and music functioning as independent musical wrok, all sounds were synchronised with music after they were imported into Wwise.


Soundtrack of "Space of Isolation" consists of 4 compositions each of them designed to play in different areas of the level in the game. Every sound effect is synchronised with music to coexist with it and togheter create independent musical work.

Batchelor's Thesis - Annihilation

This video shows final version of my bachelor's thesis project. "Annihilation" was a short game created to demonstrate how adaptive music should be implemented in games. It was also the subject of my bachelor's thesis. I created music in FL Studio and implemented it to the game using FMOD Studio. Full soundtrack is available in the music section of this website.
