Music

This section consists of my best music compositions in various genres that I created over the years and other music projects I worked on as composer or audio engineer. I create my music mainly with FL Studio, but I also use Reaper and Cubase. Reaper is used for sound design and FL Studio and Cubase for composition.







Goel Ha-Dam


In 2019 I started working on a project with my friends. The idea was to create arrangemnts, record and mix songs. We selected 8 compositions which were combined into an album. It took almost 6 months to complete this project. The end result was released in June 2019 on Spotify.




"Goel" is an short music album which is the end result of my collaboration with my friends. While working on the project I was repsonsible for recording, mixing and mastering songs. I also was repsonsible for releasing "Goel" on Spotify.



Before "Goel" was released me and singer and composer of Goel Ha-Dam recorded, mixed and published few other songs. All of them were similar to the album which we released in June 2019. These songs are available on Spotify, YouTube and other streaming platfroms.



"Bez Ciebie" is a short signle on which I worked on with one of my friends who was also a composer and singer for "Goel". In case of this song I was responsible for creating an arrangment, mixing and mastering the song. This is the last composition which I worked on as part of "Goel" project.
